Give your move a game plan.

Moving is Trouble

Most families cannot afford professional movers. When managing moving logistics and uncertainties, “Do It Yourself” movers are left to fend for themselves with little prior experience. Load.In aims to give your move a game plan by quantifying uncertainties and assisting in logistical endeavors on moving day.

Why Plan?

Inefficiency Matters

  • Inexperienced movers make inefficient packing decisions resulting in more trips than necessary.
  • Additional unexpected trips waste time and cost money.
  • Packing incorrectly can lead to damaged property and overturned trucks.
  • Damaged personal property can be devastating.
  • Accidents on the road can lead to lawsuits and loss of life.

The Solution

Introducing Load.In

Load.In gives your move a game plan by helping you find the best deal on your truck rental, providing expert truck loading instructions and giving you tools to help manage the logistics of moving day: all to give you some peace of mind during a stressful life event.

Solution Characteristics

Customer Risk

  • T-1: Artificial Intelligence prone to error due to low data.
  • T-2: UI Design not being user friendly.
  • T-3: Inability to quicky implement user feedback due to technical complexity.
  • T-4: Current technology being implemented is new and unproven in the field.

Technical Risk

  • C-1: End user disobeys the instructions given by the application.
  • C-2: User doesn’t understand how to use the application.
  • C-3: User is injured and files a lawsuit due to misuse of application.
  • C-4: User is disgruntled with the product.

Mitigation

  • T-1 Mitigation: Preload test data via internet before application is released.
  • T-2 Mitigation: Limit design changes per iteration and meet with stakeholders frequently.
  • T-3 Mitigation: Implement high level documentation and best coding practices to mitigation ease of future iterations.
  • T-4 Mitigation: Conduct a beta phase to mitigate risk of critical errors upon release.
  • C-1 Mitigation: Implement an editing feature that allows the end user to change placement of items.
  • C-2 Mitigation: Implement a tutorial on how to use the application and provide suggestions
  • C-3 Mitigation: Consult with lawyers to create a strong term of service to protect the end user and customer.
  • C-4 Mitigation: Implement a customer feedback feature that allows the users to disclose his/her issues with the applications.
